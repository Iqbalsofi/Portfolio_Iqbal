About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate (BS from Rowan University) who spent 3 years as a Software Developer and Data Analyst at Accenture and DXC Technology. That's why I went back to school for my MS in Data Science at Rowan University. I want to understand not just how to build AI systems, but why they work - from attention mechanisms in Transformers to the calculus behind gradient descent.
